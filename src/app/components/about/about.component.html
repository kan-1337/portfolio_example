About Me

Upodi is a cloud-based subscription management platform that helps businesses automate and optimize their recurring revenue streams. I use C#, TypeScript, and REST API to deliver high-quality features and enhancements that meet the user needs and business goals.


I graduated from Erhvervsakademi SydVest in June 2021 with a bachelor's degree in computer science, where I learned the fundamentals of programming, data structures, algorithms, databases, and software engineering. I also gained valuable hands-on experience from two internships at the LEGO Group and Virtual Lab ApS, where I developed full-stack solutions for mobile devices using Unity, C#, and .Net. I am passionate about learning new technologies and solving challenging problems that make a positive impact.
